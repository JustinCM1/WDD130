Content

Home page

I remember in the third grade coming home so upset at the fact that I didn't get the “artist award” at my elementary school award ceremony. This was devastating for a kid who wanted to become an artist. So I practiced a lot to get better. I remember looking at books and trying to draw the illustrations just from looking at the pictures. I would take all of my drawings and put them in a binder so I could see my progress. This website is essentially the same thing for me as the binder when I was a kid. Documenting all of the progress I have made over the past couple of months. It has been a blast kinda reliving the same excitement I felt when I was a kid and becoming better at what I do. I hope as you look through my pictures that you’ll be able to see the progress I have made and wonder what skills you can start building today.

Art

The things I draw are heavily influenced by the things that I like. A lot of things you’ll see come from tv shows, video games, and movies. Not only will you get to see some cool drawings by me but I’ll also be giving a short summary of the show and the character that I drew! It's a win-win situation.

Reviews

I pride myself on having the best opinions of anyone that I know. So this review section will help you make your own decision to watch/play the media that I drew. I will be using a simple scale using the numbers 1 through 10 on why I believe it deserves this score. You can rest easy now that you know that you won’t be caught in the endless loop of scrolling through Netflix or GameStop always thinking to yourself, “What am I gonna watch/play next?”
